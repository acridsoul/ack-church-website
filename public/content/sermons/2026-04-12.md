1. Encourage daily prayer and devotion
2. Be role models
3. Family is the most important place children get values
4. Practice pillar mentorship
5. You should not despise children
6. Love the Lord your God with all your heart, with all your soul, and with all your strength
7. Children are a gift from the Lord; they are a real blessing
8. Tie God's laws on your arms and wear them on your foreheads as a reminder
9. Have hope and trust in God
10. Teach children to love God's house
11. Jesus grew both in body and in wisdom, gaining favour with God and people
