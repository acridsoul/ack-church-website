## English Service Notes

**Preacher:** L/R Kungu

**Bible Verses:** Isaiah 11:1-5, John 13:1-5

**Theme:** The Spirit of Servant Leadership

### Sermon Notes

1. **From a Stump, New Life Grows** - Isaiah prophesied that a shoot would come up from the stump of Jesse. Even when things look dead and finished, God can bring forth new life and new leadership.

2. **The Spirit of God Equips True Leaders** - The Spirit of the Lord rested on the coming Messiah — the spirit of wisdom, understanding, counsel, power, knowledge and the fear of the Lord. Godly leadership is Spirit-led.

3. **True Leadership Sees Beyond the Surface** - The Messiah "will not judge by what he sees with his eyes or decide by what he hears with his ears." Spirit-led leaders look beyond appearance to the truth.

4. **Righteousness and Justice Define Godly Leadership** - He will judge the poor with fairness and defend the needy. Those who lead in God's kingdom must champion justice for the vulnerable.

5. **Jesus Defined Leadership by Serving** - On the night before His death, Jesus took a towel and basin and washed His disciples' feet. The greatest leader became the lowest servant.

6. **Love That Goes All the Way** - John 13 tells us Jesus loved His own "to the full" — to the very end, completely and without holding back. That is the standard of Christian love.

7. **We Are Called to Pick Up the Towel** - Jesus said, "I have set you an example that you should do as I have done for you." True greatness in God's kingdom is measured by how we serve others.

## Kikuyu Service Notes

**Mũrutani:** L/R Lydia

**Gũthoma Ibuku:** Isaiah 11:1-5, John 13:1-5

**Theme:** The Spirit of Servant Leadership

### Sermon Notes

1. **God Brings Life from Dead Places** - The shoot from Jesse's stump teaches us that God can restore and raise up what the world has written off. Do not despair over difficult situations — God is still at work.

2. **God's Leaders are Filled with His Spirit** - The Spirit of wisdom, understanding and the fear of the Lord rests on those God anoints. Seek the filling of the Holy Spirit in your life and leadership.

3. **Fear of the Lord is the Beginning of True Wisdom** - The Messiah delights in the fear of the Lord. Reverence for God is the foundation of every good and godly leader.

4. **Jesus Humbled Himself to Serve** - The King of Kings knelt before His disciples and washed their feet. No work of service is beneath those who follow Christ.

5. **The Greatest Act of Love is Selfless Service** - Jesus showed His love not only with words but with action. True love serves even when it is inconvenient or costly.

6. **Lead Others the Way Christ Led You** - We are called to serve our families, our church and our community with the same humility and love that Christ showed His disciples.
