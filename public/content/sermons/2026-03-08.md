## English Service Notes

**Preacher:** L/R Kungu

**Bible Verses:** Daniel 6:10-22, Luke 18:1-8

**Theme:** Faithful and Persistent Prayer

### Sermon Notes

1. Daniel prayed three times a day despite the king's decree — his devotion to God was greater than his fear of man.
2. God delivered Daniel from the lions' den; our God is able to rescue those who trust in Him.
3. Jesus taught the parable of the persistent widow so that we would always pray and not give up.
4. If an unjust judge answered the widow, how much more will our loving Father answer when we cry out to Him?
