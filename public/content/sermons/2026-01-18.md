## English Service Notes

**Preacher:** Ann Muruga

**Bible Verses:** Deuteronomy 28:1-14, Mark 2:1-12

### Sermon Notes

1. **Obedience Brings Blessings** - Deuteronomy reminds us that when we diligently obey God's commands, He will set us high above all nations and bless us in every area of life.

2. **God's Promises Are Conditional** - The blessings of Deuteronomy 28 are conditional upon our obedience to God's voice and careful observance of His commandments.

3. **Blessings in Every Sphere** - God promises blessings in the city and in the country, in our coming in and going out, in our basket and kneading bowl.

4. **Jesus Has Authority to Forgive** - Mark 2 shows us that Jesus has the authority to forgive sins, which was a revolutionary concept that challenged the religious leaders of His time.

5. **Faith Brings Healing** - The paralyzed man was healed not just because of his own faith, but because of the faith of his friends who brought him to Jesus.

6. **Physical and Spiritual Healing** - Jesus demonstrated that He can heal both physical ailments and spiritual brokenness, showing His complete authority over all aspects of our lives.

7. **The Son of Man Has Authority** - Jesus declared that the Son of Man has authority on earth to forgive sins, establishing His divine identity and power.

## Kikuyu Service Notes

**Mũrutani:** Lay Reader Damaris

**Gũthoma Ibuku:** Deuteronomy 28:1-14, Mark 2:1-12

### Maandĩko ma Ũhoro

1. **Ũgũthii Gũtũũra Nĩgũtũũra Ngai** - Mũhũbĩri 28 nĩatũrĩkanagia atĩ tũgũthii gũtũũra maathani ma Ngai, nĩagũtũũra thĩinĩ wa maũndũ mothe.

2. **Maũndũ ma Ngai Nĩmaagĩrĩire Ũgũthii** - Tha cia Ngai nĩmaagĩrĩire gũthii gũtũũra maathani make na gũtũũra ciugo ciake.

3. **Tha Thĩinĩ wa Maũndũ Mothe** - Ngai nĩatũhũtaga tha thĩinĩ wa gũthii, gũgũka, na maũndũ mothe ma mũtũũro witũ.

4. **Jesũ Nĩarĩ na Ũhoti wa Gũthũũria** - Mark 2 nĩatũhonagia atĩ Jesũ arĩ na ũhoti wa gũthũũria maũndũ ma ngoro, ũndũ ũrĩa warahũthagĩra andũ a thogora.

5. **Ũkĩrĩru Nĩũgũtũũra** - Mũndũ ũrĩa warĩ na mũtũũro nĩaathũũrũtwo ti nĩ ũndũ wa ũkĩrĩru wake no nĩ ũndũ wa ũkĩrĩru wa arũme ake.

6. **Gũthũũria kwa Mwĩrĩ na kwa Ngoro** - Jesũ nĩaonyetia atĩ angĩthũũra maũndũ ma mwĩrĩ na ma ngoro, aonyetia ũhoti wake mũnene.

7. **Mũrũ wa Mũndũ Arĩ na Ũhoti** - Jesũ nĩarĩkanagia atĩ Mũrũ wa Mũndũ arĩ na ũhoti thĩinĩ wa thĩ wa gũthũũria maũndũ ma ngoro, aonyetia ũrĩa we nĩ Ngai.
