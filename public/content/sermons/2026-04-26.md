## English Service Notes

**Preacher:** Gabriel Muriuki

**Bible Verses:** 2 Chronicles 20:22, Revelation 4:8-9

**Theme:** Praise and Worship as a Weapon

### Sermon Notes

1. The moment you start praising the Lord, He sets ambush to all your enemies and troubles.
2. Have you been using praise and worship? (Worship shifts an atmosphere.)
3. Praising and worshiping God is a weapon that sets even titles.
4. You must be consistent in praise and worship.
5. Worshipping God should be your lifestyle.
