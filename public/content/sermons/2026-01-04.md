## English Service Notes

## New Beginnings in Christ

As we step into 2026, we are reminded that in Christ, all things become new. This is a season for fresh starts and renewed commitment to our faith.

### Key Points

1. **A Time for Everything** - Ecclesiastes reminds us that there is a season for every activity under heaven. God's timing is perfect, and He orchestrates all things for our good.

2. **New Creation in Christ** - 2 Corinthians 5:17 declares that if anyone is in Christ, they are a new creation. The old has gone, the new has come!

3. **Walking in Newness of Life** - Our new beginning is not just a calendar change, but a spiritual transformation that should reflect in our daily walk.

### Application

- Surrender your plans and dreams to God this new year
- Let go of past failures and embrace God's grace
- Set spiritual goals for growth in prayer, study, and service

> "Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here!" - 2 Corinthians 5:17

## Kikuyu Service Notes

## Mĩambĩrĩrio Mĩerũ thĩinĩ wa Kristũ

Tũkĩingĩra mwaka wa 2026, nĩtũrĩkanagio atĩ thĩinĩ wa Kristũ, maũndũ mothe nĩmacokaga kũrĩ maerũ. Ĩno nĩ hĩndĩ ya mĩambĩrĩrio mĩerũ na gũcokereria ũkĩrĩru witũ.

### Maũndũ ma Bata

1. **Hĩndĩ ya Ũndũ o Wothe** - Mũhũbĩri atũrĩkanagia atĩ kũrĩ hĩndĩ ya ũndũ o wothe thĩ. Matukũ ma Ngai nĩ mega, na aratũũrĩria maũndũ mothe nĩ ũndũ wa wega witũ.

2. **Gũumbwo Kwerũ thĩinĩ wa Kristũ** - 2 Akorinto 5:17 ĩrĩkanagia atĩ mũndũ o wothe ũrĩ thĩinĩ wa Kristũ, nĩ kĩũmbe kĩerũ. Cia tene nĩciathira, njeũ nĩciũkĩte!

3. **Gũthiĩ na Mũtũũro Mũerũ** - Mwambĩrĩrio witũ mũerũ ti wa karenda, no nĩ wa kũgarũrwo kwa ngoro kũrĩa kwaagĩrĩire kũoneka mũtũũro-inĩ witũ wa o mũthenya.

### Kũhũthĩra

- Tũũria Ngai mĩrĩĩri yaku na iroto ciaku mwaka ũyũ mũerũ
- Tiga cia tene na gwĩkĩra tha cia Ngai
- Ĩka maũndũ ma gũkũra thĩinĩ wa kũhoya, gũthoma, na gũtungatĩra

> "Nĩ ũndũ ũcio, mũndũ o wothe arĩ thĩinĩ wa Kristũ, nĩ kĩũmbe kĩerũ: cia tene nĩciathira, njeũ nĩciũkĩte!" - 2 Akorinto 5:17
