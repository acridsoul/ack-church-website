## English Service Notes

**Preacher:** Vicar Henry

**First reading:** Isaiah 53:1-12

**Second reading:** (none for this service)

**Gospel:** Mark 16:1-8

**Theme:** Hope Beyond Suffering

### Sermon Notes

1. Jesus took our suffering on himself and this brought us peace.
2. The Prince of Peace is Jesus Christ.
3. We were healed because of the pain that Jesus endured.
4. Have strength and do not be afraid.
5. In sickness and in pain, turn to Jesus.
6. Your suffering will be no more!
7. There is hope beyond suffering.
8. At the point of your pain, may you meet with Jesus.
